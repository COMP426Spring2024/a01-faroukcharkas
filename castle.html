Castle

Now about the spooy spooky castle...

The Facts

  • In the heart of a world shrouded in the mists of time, the Order of Gimghoul holds court within the enigmatic walls of Hippol Castle, a realm more commonly whispered among the initiated as Gimghoul Castle.
  • Edward Wray Martin, a visionary law student and founding soul of the Order, ignited by the fires of his passion for Arthurian legends, dreamt a dream bold and grand. His voice, fervent and unwavering, conjured from the echoes of medieval ballads, envisioned a fortress not of this age: a sanctuary for knights of shadow and light, amidst a world blind to the magic that dwells in the unseen.
  • Martin's dream was a citadel perched on the brink of eternity, a "great gloomy pile" standing as a guardian over the abyss, a stage upon which the secret dances of the Order could unfold, shrouded from the gaze of mortals.
  • The origins of this castle, veiled in the whispers of the past, are a tale of mystery and marvel. Crafted in 1926 by the hands of Waldensian stone-masons from Valdese, North Carolina, or perhaps by French artisans, the castle rose, stone by stone, a testament to the arcane, enveloped in tales of its creation that span from 1924 to the ambiguous depths of the 1920s.
  • This fortress, a labor of love and secrecy, demanded a king's ransom to emerge from the earth, its construction devouring over $50,000—a sum that whispers of fortunes in today's silver and gold.
  • To breathe life into their monumental dream, the Order parted with 35 acres of land, sowing the seeds of the Gimghoul Neighborhood Historic District, a legacy carved from the very essence of their ambition.
  • Set beyond the confines of mere academia, at the terminus of Gimghoul Road and in the shadow of the Old Chapel Hill Cemetery, this castle stands as a monument to the unseen, its dominion marked by a valuation soaring beyond the realm of a million dreams.
  • Legend holds that beneath its foundations, or perhaps within a stone's throw, lies the heart of the saga—Peter Dromgoole's final rest, his spirit wandering the castle grounds, a sentinel bound to the realm of the living by the crimson stain of his blood on Dromgoole Rock, an eternal testament to a tale of love, loss, and the undying echo of legend.
