The Duel

Learn about the duel that started it all... (RIP Peter)

What Happened?

  • There was once a boy named Peter Dromgoole
  • He was in love with Miss Fanny from Chapel Hill
  • Peter and another suitor dueled over Fanny, and Peter was killed
  • Legend says Peter was buried under a rock stained with his blood
  • One story claims Fanny prayed for his return daily at the castle
  • Another version says Fanny witnessed Peter's death, marking the rock with his blood
  • Peter Dromgoole was a real person who disappeared after failing to enter the University of North Carolina in 1833
  • The tale might be linked to a duel fought by Peter's uncle, George C. Dromgoole, in 1837
  • Ghimghoul Castle is associated with the secret Order of the Gimghoul, founded in 1889
  • The castle, initially called Hippol Castle, was built in the early 1920s with 1,300 tons of stone by French artisans
